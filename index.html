About Medhavardhan Foundation

Medhavardhan Foundation believes in the conscious efforts and for that we prepare our beneficiaries in such a manner that they could stand at their best in the present competitive world. We know the need of the time and that is why Medhavardhan Foundation works in every prospect to sharpen and nourishing the talents of individual in order to create that extra effects and glow in their personality and career. We know the value of time and the need of the children. Thus we try to merge in the needs of the children with their rights.

In order to protect those rights of the underprivileged, we train them to achieve the desired level through the technique of capacity building and empowerment. This whole sequence leads to an inclusive perfect strategy of socio-economic transformation.

So, Medhavardhan Foundation is an ultimate package of livelihood though different integrated programmed with the perks attached i.e. Self-efficiency and self-sufficiency.
